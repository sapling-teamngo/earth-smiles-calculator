Implementation of Semicircular Bunds for Sustainable Water Harvesting in Al Mafraq, Jordan

Authors: Omar Yaseen, Mariam Najajrah, Hanadi Yosuf
Supervisor: Dr. Amjad Shatnawi
Institution: [Your University/Organization]

Abstract

This research investigates the implementation of semicircular bunds (earth smiles) in the semi-arid region of Al Mafraq, Jordan. Using design parameters from WOCAT and FAO methodologies, we propose an optimized system for water harvesting...

Study Area: Al Mafraq City

Coordinates: 32.3419° N, 36.2020° E

Average Rainfall: 150-200 mm/year

Soil Type: predominantly loamy soil

Slope: 3-8% gentle slopes

Methodology

Based on WOCAT Benin parameters adapted for Jordanian conditions:

  • Reduced diameter to 3.0m due to lower rainfall
  • Increased organic matter application
  • Modified spacing for optimal water retention
