Structure Diameter Calculation

Equation: D = 4.0 × Sf × min(Rf, 1.2)

Where:

  • Dbase = 4.0 m (Base diameter from WOCAT Benin)
  • Sf = Slope factor (0.6-1.0)
  • Rf = Rainfall factor (rainfall/1025)

Source: WOCAT Benin Case Study, Pages 1-2
